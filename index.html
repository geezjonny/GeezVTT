✦ Features & Maneuvers
▶
Second Wind
Bonus Action▶
Heal 1d10 + 3 HP. 2 uses — regain 1 on a Short Rest, both on a Long Rest.
Action Surge
Short Rest▶
Take one additional Action this turn. Cannot be a Magic action — you can't use it to cast a spell.
Precision Attack
Maneuver · 1 SD▶
When you miss with a weapon attack, expend 1 Superiority Die and add the roll to your attack roll — potentially turning it into a hit. Can be used after seeing the roll but before the DM says it hits or misses.
Riposte
Maneuver · Reaction · 1 SD▶
When a creature misses you with a melee attack, use your Reaction to make one melee weapon attack against it. On a hit, add 1d8 to the damage. Punishes enemies who whiff.
Trip Attack
Maneuver · 1 SD▶
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, expend 1 SD and add +1d8 damage. If the target is Large or smaller, it must succeed on a STR save DC 13 or be knocked Prone. Your allies get advantage on their attacks while it's prone.
Menacing Attack
Maneuver · 1 SD▶
When you hit with a weapon attack, expend 1 SD to add +1d8 damage. The target must make a WIS save DC 13 or become Frightened of you until the end of your next turn (disadvantage on attacks and checks while it can see you, can't move toward you).
Know Your Enemy
Battle Master · Passive▶
If you spend 1 minute observing or interacting with a creature outside of combat, the DM tells you if the creature is equal, superior, or inferior to you in two of the following: STR, DEX, CON, AC, HP, class levels (if any).
Maneuver Superiority DC
DC 13▶
8 + proficiency bonus (2) + STR modifier (3) = DC 13. Applies to Trip Attack and Menacing Attack saves.

Cold Breath Weapon
Silver Dragonborn · 2/LR▶
When you take the Attack action, replace one attack with a breath weapon: 15ft cone or 30ft line (5ft wide) — DEX save DC 13 or 1d10 Cold damage (half on save). 2 uses per Long Rest.
Cold Resistance
Silver Dragonborn · Passive▶
You have Resistance to Cold damage from your Silver Dragon ancestry. Cold spells and icy environments deal half damage to you.
Draconic Flight
Silver Dragonborn · 1/LR▶
Once per Long Rest as a Bonus Action: gain temporary flight for 10 minutes (Fly Speed = 30ft). Retracting the wings is free; also ends if you are Incapacitated.
